CamSoda is a live streaming webcam platform.CamSoda was started by Edgar Fernandez in 2014. The video streaming platform provides for adult webcams and non-adult lifestreams. CamSoda was the first to offer 360-degree virtual reality live streams. They offer products involving virtual reality touch-screen technology, and products powered by Bitcoin and Ethereum.The company and their products have been mention in various media outlets including The Howard Stern Show, PC Magazine, Glamour, and Cosmopolitan.In March 2018, the platform featured a live broadcast of an MMA event.
