The Inland Revenue Department (IRD) of Hong Kong Government is one of Hong Kong government departments which is responsible for the administration of the following Hong Kong ordinances on taxes and duties and the related rules and regulations:
Betting Duty Ordinance Cap.108
Business Registration Ordinance Cap.310
Estate Duty Ordinance Cap.111
Hotel Accommodation Tax Ordinance Cap.348
Inland Revenue Ordinance Cap.112
Stamp Duty Ordinance Cap.117
Tax Reserve Certificates Ordinance Cap.289


The Inland Revenue Department is headquartered at the eponymous Revenue Tower in Wanchai. Before that, the department was located at Windsor House.
