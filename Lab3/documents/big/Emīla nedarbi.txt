Emīla nedarbi (Russian: "Проделки сорванца" - Tricks of rascal) is a 1985 Latvian TV-film about the Astrid Lindgren character, Emil i Lönneberga. It was produced by Rīgas kinostudija.
The film was awarded the Latvian National Film Prize Lielais Kristaps in 1985.[1]
