Rabén & Sjögren is a book publishing company in Sweden. It was established in 1942 by Hans Rabén and Carl-Olof Sjögren. Since 1998 it is part of Norstedts förlag.
The publishing focus is on children's and youth literature.  Rabén & Sjögren was very successfully publishing the books of Astrid Lindgren. Other authors include Enid Blyton and Jostein Gaarder.
