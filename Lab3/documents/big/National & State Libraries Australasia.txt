National & State Libraries Australasia (NSLA) is a consortium that represents the national, state and territory libraries of Australia and New Zealand.
The libraries collaborate on and support working groups addressing issues including: archival collections, collection development, marketing, copyright, collecting and preserving digital content, collections and services focussing on Indigenous Australians, picture collections, literacy and collection storage management.NSLA also compiles annual Australian public library statistics.
