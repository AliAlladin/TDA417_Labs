Euclid Kiriakovich Kyurdzidis (Russian: Эвклид Кириакович Кюрдзидис),  Euclid Gurdjiev (Gr. Ευκλείδης Κιουρτζίδης του Κυριάκου, born 22 February 1968 in Yessentuki) is a Russian actor, best known for his role as Ruslan Shamayev in Voyna. He was born to ethnic Greek parents in Yessentuki, Stavropol Krai. He has two brothers and an older sister.
