Kirovsky District (Russian: Ки́ровский райо́н) is an administrative district (raion), one of the twenty-six in Stavropol Krai, Russia. Municipally, it is incorporated as Kirovsky Municipal District. It is located in the south of the krai. The area of the district is 1,386 square kilometers (535 sq mi). Its administrative center is the town of Novopavlovsk. Population: 71,723 (2010 Census); 67,317 (2002 Census); 55,074 (1989 Census). The population of Novopavlovsk accounts for 37.0% of the district's total population.
