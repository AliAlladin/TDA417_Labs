The Journal of Political Economy is a bimonthly peer-reviewed academic journal published by the University of Chicago Press. It covers both theoretical and empirical economics. It was established in 1892 by James Laurence Laughlin. In January 2020, the journal will move to a monthly publication schedule.Its current editor-in-chief is Harald Uhlig (University of Chicago).
