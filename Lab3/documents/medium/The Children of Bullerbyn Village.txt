The Children of Bullerbyn Village (Swedish: Alla vi barn i Bullerbyn) is a 1960 TV film re-edited from the 1960 Swedish TV series of the same name and directed by Olle Hellbom. It was entered into the 2nd Moscow International Film Festival.
